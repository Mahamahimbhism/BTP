Welcome to the Eriksen Flanker Task

Instructions:

In this task, you will see a row of 5 arrows on the screen.

Your task is to respond to the direction of the CENTER arrow only, ignoring the surrounding arrows.

Response Keys:

  • Press ← (Left Arrow) if the center arrow points LEFT
  • Press → (Right Arrow) if the center arrow points RIGHT

Three Types of Trials:

1. Congruent Trials

→ → → → →

All arrows point in the same direction

Response: Press →

2. Incongruent Trials

← ← → ← ←

Flanking arrows point in the opposite direction

Response: Press → (center arrow)

3. Neutral Trials

□ □ → □ □

Flanking stimuli have no directional information

Response: Press → (center arrow)

⚠️ Important: Please respond as quickly and accurately as possible!
